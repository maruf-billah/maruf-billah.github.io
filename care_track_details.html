CARE Track: Transforming CARE Bangladesh’s Project Implementation and Service Delivery

In today’s fast-changing development and humanitarian landscape, data-driven decision-making is essential. CARE Bangladesh has taken a bold step with CARE Track, a centralized and robust Management Information System (MIS) that optimizes operations, strengthens service delivery, and turns information into action. With more than 20 specialized modules, CARE Track streamlines everything from data collection to reporting — a single, powerful platform for all projects.

How CARE Track Transforms Project Implementation

Before CARE Track, teams relied on fragmented tools and manual processes for data collection, tracking, and reporting — creating delays, inefficiencies, and limited visibility. CARE Track addresses these challenges by mainstreaming data across all projects, enabling real-time monitoring, delivering 100% progress tracking for faster decisions, and strengthening program quality with transparent, automated reporting. The result: faster service delivery with higher accountability and measurable impact.

The Overall Process

1

Start with Accurate Profiles

Beneficiary profile data is collected through the Survey & Data Collection module, establishing a clean source of truth from day one.

Survey Enrollment
2

Enroll & Organize

Individuals are formally enrolled into programs and organized into groups (e.g., CSGs, YSLAs) using Group Management—making participation structured and traceable.

Group Mgmt
3

Track Every Activity

Trainings, capacity development, events, and distributions (cash/kind) are recorded in their dedicated modules—each entry linked back to participants and groups.

Training Capacity Dev Events Distribution
4

Close the Loop with Communities

Feedback and complaints are logged through FAM/CFRM for responsive action, while knowledge and evidence are captured in KML for learning and adaptation.

FAM/CFRM KML
5

Unified Participants, Multiple Sources

For any activity, participants can be pulled from the central enrollment list, existing groups, or directly from survey data—maximizing flexibility and coverage.

Groups Central Registry
6

Automated Indicators & Reporting

All activity data is mapped to indicators. CARE Track automatically calculates achievements for any period and compiles reports and dashboards—no manual crunching required.

DIP/IPTT Dashboards Reports
